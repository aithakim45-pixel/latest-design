Frequently Asked Questions

Is BOM Extractor free of charge?

BOM Extractor is free of charge for all GSD IDEA Customers with an IDEA StatiCa Enterprise license and active Maintenance. Others can contact our sales representative.

How much time will I save using BOM Extractor?

Expected time saving for each 10 connections is 2 hours.

I am an Enterprise customer, who in my company can use it?

Everybody who requested BOM Extractor via the form and is a Commercial License user of IDEA StatiCa.

Do I need local admin rights to install BOM Extractor?

Local admin rights are not required to launch. BOM Extractor is a click-and-use type of application.

What are the minimum requirements to run BOM Extractor?

Same as for the IDEA StatiCa application. It requires 120MBs of HDD space.

Do I need IDEA StatiCa installed on my PC?

Yes, it is mandatory.

What version of IDEA StatiCa do I need to have?

BOM Extractor requires the corresponding version to be installed on your PC (currently v25.1 or v26.0). However, you can extract reports from .ideacon files created in older versions like V24.1.

How can users log in and use BOM Extractor?

1. Launch the app. 2. Login with the email you use to access IDEA StatiCa. 3. Check your mailbox for an OTP. 4. Insert the OTP into the login screen.
